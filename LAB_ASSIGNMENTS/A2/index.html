About the App

This To-Do List application helps you organize your daily tasks, prioritize important items, and track your progress efficiently.

With a clean and intuitive interface, you can easily manage all your tasks in one place.

Features

  • Add, edit, and delete tasks
  • Mark tasks as complete
  • Star important tasks
  • Categorize tasks (Work, Home, College)
  • Track progress with visual indicators
  • Responsive design for all devices
